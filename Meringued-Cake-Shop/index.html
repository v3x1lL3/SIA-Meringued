Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about ordering custom cakes

How far in advance should I order?

We recommend placing orders at least 1-3 weeks in advance for custom cakes.

Do you accept rush orders?

Rush orders may be accepted on a case-by-case basis, but we require at least 3 days' notice for any rush booking to check schedule and ingredient availability.

All confirmed rush and regular orders are final — no refunds.

What flavors do you offer?

We offer a wide variety of flavors including chocolate moist, vanilla, and mocha.We can also create custom flavor combinations to match your preferences!

Do you deliver?

Yes! We offer delivery services within the local area. Delivery fees vary based on location. Pickup is also available at our shop.

Do you offer consultations?

Absolutely! We provide design consultations via communication platforms such as Facebook or Messenger. For more details, please check the Facebook logo link below the website page footer.
