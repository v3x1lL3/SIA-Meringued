Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about ordering custom cakes

How far in advance should I order?

We recommend placing orders at least 2-3 weeks in advance for custom cakes. For larger or more complex designs, please allow 4-6 weeks.

What flavors do you offer?

We offer a wide variety of flavors including vanilla, chocolate, red velvet, lemon, strawberry, and many more. We can also create custom flavor combinations to match your preferences!

Do you deliver?

Yes! We offer delivery services within the local area. Delivery fees vary based on location. Pickup is also available at our shop.

Can I see a mockup before ordering?

Absolutely! We provide design consultations and can create mockups of your cake design before you confirm your order.
